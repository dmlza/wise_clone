Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the bridge.

1 Will Wisecracker be available in the Philippines?
Absolutely. The Philippines is one of our launch markets. You'll be able to send PHP in and out within minutes using our partner rails.
2 Who is BlockShoals Technologies?
BlockShoals Technologies is the engineering team behind Wisecracker. We build compliant fiat-to-crypto infrastructure for global remittance corridors.
3 Why is Wisecracker the best bridge for crypto traders?
We combine Wise-style transparent pricing with Binance-grade execution. No slippage, no hidden spreads, and settlement in under 30 minutes.
4 How to buy Bitcoin instantly on Wisecracker?
Head to the dashboard, enter the USD amount you want to convert, and confirm. Your BTC lands in your wallet within minutes — no order book, no waiting.
5 How to track cryptocurrency prices in real-time?
Our dashboard features a live BTC/USDT candlestick chart powered by real market data. Toggle floating rate mode on the converter to see prices move in real-time.
6 How to trade fiat to crypto cleanly?
Select your fiat currency, enter the amount, choose BTC or any supported crypto, and hit send. We handle the conversion, compliance, and settlement.
